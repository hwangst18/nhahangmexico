Our Story

Discover the passion and heritage behind Tica's Tacos

A Family Tradition Since 1985

Founded by Angel Tica, our restaurant brings authentic Mexican flavors to your table. Every taco tells a story of tradition, passion, and the rich culinary heritage of Mexico.

We use only the freshest ingredients, locally sourced whenever possible, and prepare everything from scratch daily. Our recipes have been passed down through generations, keeping the authentic taste of Mexico alive in every bite.
